# Lore

## The World

Welcome to the Reach! This is an old and storied land. 

## East: The Order of the Black Falcons

Order of the Falcon territory. Younger, more militarised, built around chapter houses and fortified positions. Still consolidating after pushing east a generation ago. The Order is lean, and relies on harsh discipline and rigorous training to make up for the lack of equipment. Low tier units are more experienced and seen more combat than most other units of their rank. Falcon units are typically equipped with heavier weapons, and their martial philosophy centers around striking hard and first.

## West: The Lion Kingdom

Lion Kingdom heartland. River valleys, farmland, established roads and towns. Old money, institutional power, slow to change. While the original power of the land, the Lion Kingdom has seen a decline as political infighting amongst the nobles and a failed campaign against the Order of the Black Falcons has resulted in a shrinkage of its lands and prestige. Lion Kingdom units rely on equipment rather than training to win, but are famously well balanced.

## The North

The Hinterlands. Ungoverned. Dense forest giving way to open moorland. Bandit clans, displaced soldiers, minor lords with no real allegiance. The Lion Kingdom and the Order both raid it for mercenaries and neither wants to pay to pacify it. 

## The Center

The contested zone. Crestfall and the other free cities sit here. Trade routes, minor lords playing both sides, the Merchant Houses operating in the gaps between the two powers. Merchant Houses tend to utilise unconventional tactics and units, preferring surgical strikes rather than slugging it out. 

## The Monsters

Old fortresses, abandoned towers, forgotten villages dot the landscape. These from the lairs of monsters of every shape and size.
