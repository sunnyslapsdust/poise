# Rules

## Overview

Poise is a skirmish wargame played between two or more players, each commanding a warband of units on a gridded battlefield.

## Poise

Every unit has a **Current Poise (CP)** and a **Max Poise (MP)**. CP is spent to take actions and recovered over time. A unit reduced to 0 MP is Knocked Out.

## Actions

On your turn you may spend CP freely across any of your units.

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Attack | 2 CP (or as listed) |
| Recover | Free once per turn |

## Combat

To attack, an attacker must roll their attack die. This is dependent on their Current Poise.

If the attack die is equal to or more than half of the defender's Current Poise rounded up, the defender's current poise is reduced by the attacker's Damage value. If it is equal or more than the Current Poise, the Defender is Hit.

When a unit is hit, reduce its Max Poise by the attacker's Damage value. A unit whose Max Poise reaches 0 is Knocked Out and removed from play. A unit whose Max Poise is 1 is Stunned.


## Spellcasting

Wizard units may cast spells by spending CP. Each spell has a Target Number (TN) — roll an attack die based on your current CP and meet or beat it to succeed.

Some spells are limited to specific wizard archetypes.

A wizard has a limited number of spell slots. The first time a wizard attempts to cast a spell will fill up that spell slot for the rest of the battle. Concentrating reduces a spell's TN by 1 each turn.
