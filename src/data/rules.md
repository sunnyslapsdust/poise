# Rules

## Overview

Poise is a skirmish wargame played between two or more players, each commanding a warband of units on a gridded battlefield.

## Poise

Every unit has a **Current Poise (CP)** and a **Max Poise (MP)**. CP is spent to take actions and recovered over time. A unit reduced to 0 MP is Knocked Out.

## Actions

On your turn you may spend CP freely across any of your units.

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Attack | 2 CP (or as listed) |
| Recover | Free once per turn |

## Combat

When a unit is hit, reduce its Max Poise by the attacker's Damage value. A unit whose Max Poise reaches 0 is Knocked Out and removed from play.

## Spellcasting

Wizard units may cast spells by spending CP. Each spell has a Target Number (TN) — roll your spell die and meet or beat it to succeed.

A wizard has a limited number of spell slots. Each cast occupies one slot until the wizard's next turn. Concentrating reduces a spell's TN by 1 each turn.
